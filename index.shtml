Welcome to Cardinal Cards!

I am excited to launch my revamped online greeting card store! This new store uses a supplier who will print and ship the cards to you.

The benefits of this new store are:

  • The cards are 5"x7" and have a beautiful, glossy, professional look.
  • Shipping is FREE for any order of 5+ cards (in the US only).
  • You can send a card directly to the recipient!
  • Cards can now be ordered from or shipped to any country.
  • Highly affordable prices! Be sure to look for the discount code in the banner at the top of the store to save even more on your order.
  • In-store pick-up is available from Target, but card quality may slightly decrease.
